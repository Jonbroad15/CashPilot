# Mission

CashPilot is a personal budgeting application that simplifies and automates personal finance management.

## Goals

**Seamless account connectivity.** Connect directly to users' bank and spending accounts so that transactions are ingested automatically as purchases are made — no manual CSV exports required.

**Intelligent categorization.** Classify every transaction into a meaningful spending category (groceries, restaurants, travel, etc.) using machine learning, with minimal user intervention.

**Monthly habit tracking.** Track spending by category across months, surfacing trends and patterns that help users understand where their money goes.

**Target-setting and accountability.** Help users set realistic monthly spending targets per category based on their historical habits, and alert them when they are on track to overspend.

**Savings goal management.** Let users define savings goals (emergency fund, vacation, down payment, etc.), track progress against them, and understand how current spending habits affect the timeline to reach each goal.

**Investment advice.** Analyse cash flow and savings capacity to provide personalized, data-driven recommendations on investment opportunities — helping users put idle money to work in line with their risk tolerance and financial goals.

**Proactive recommendations.** Surface actionable, data-driven insights — not just raw numbers — so users can make better financial decisions without having to dig through spreadsheets.

## Who It's For

Individuals who want visibility and control over their personal finances without the friction of manual bookkeeping or the cost of a full financial advisory service.
